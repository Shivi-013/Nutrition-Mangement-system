Diet Plan for Weight Loss

Follow this diet plan to achieve your weight loss goals:

  • Calorie Control: Consume fewer calories than your body burns.

  • Lean Proteins: Include poultry, fish, tofu, and legumes.

  • Healthy Fats: Opt for avocados, nuts, and olive oil in moderation.

  • Low-Fat Dairy: Choose options like skim milk, yogurt, and cheese.

  • Veggies Priority: Emphasize non-starchy vegetables.

  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water and consider herbal teas.

  • Whole Grains: Prefer quinoa, barley, and bulgur over refined grains.
