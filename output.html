Diet Plan for Weight Gain

Follow this diet plan to achieve your weight gain goals:

  • Consume more calories than your body burns.

  • Include protein-rich foods such as lean meats, dairy, and legumes.

  • Incorporate healthy fats like avocados, nuts, and olive oil.

  • Full-fat dairy, including milk, yogurt and cheese

  • Whole grains like whole-wheat bread, whole-wheat pasta, brown rice.

  • Eat complex carbohydrates such as whole grains and vegetables.

  • Stay hydrated and consider adding smoothies or shakes to increase calorie intake.
